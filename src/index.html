About our salon


Welcome to Rainbow Nails! A newly established professional nail care salon located near the Gloucester Quays and the historic docks. Our friendly salon provides you with a relaxing environment to feel pampered in. Founded in May 2018 by Kelly Li. Kelly has over 3 years experience as a nail technician. Kelly's goal is to provide professional and affordable nail treatments to suit everyone.

Our salon is fully air-conditioned for your comfort.
